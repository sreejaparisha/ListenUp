Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime without any penalties. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

+

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. The price difference will be adjusted in your next billing cycle.

Do I get a free trial?

+

Yes! Individual plans come with 1 month free for new users. Family plans also include a free trial period.

Can I share my account with others?

+

Yes, with the Family plan, you can add up to 6 family members to your account. Each member gets their own personalized experience.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets. Your payment information is always secure.

How many devices can I use?

+

You can use your Premium account on multiple devices, but only play music on one device at a time per account.
