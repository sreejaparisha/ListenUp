Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? ▼
The free trial gives you full access to all Premium features for 1 month. No credit card required for new users. After the trial ends, your subscription will automatically continue at the regular monthly rate.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes! You can cancel your Premium subscription anytime from your account settings. Your access will continue until your current billing cycle ends. No hidden fees or long-term commitments.
What's the difference between Individual and Family plans? ▼
Individual plan is for one person. Family plan is for up to 6 family members living at the same address. Family plan includes parental controls and a shared family activity feed.
Do I need to use my own payment method or can I use a gift card? ▼
You can use various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets, and more. We also plan to support Premium gift cards in the future.
Is the Student plan verified? ▼
Yes, verification is required for the Student plan. You'll need a valid student ID or current .edu email address to qualify for the 50% discount.
